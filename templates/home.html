About PyTea

Hello there! PyTea is a local start up business by 3 Singaporean students who are bubble tea enthusiasts. The name PyTea cleverly combines the word “Python” with “tea”, creating a playful and tech-inspired name for our bubble tea shop. Additionally, our name also sounds like “buy tea” when you pronounce it.

Our Drinks

At PyTea, we believe that crafting the perfect bubble tea is an art form. Every drink we create is a masterpiece, carefully curated to provide you with an unforgettable taste experience. Our commitment to quality starts with the finest, freshest ingredients that nature has to offer.

Our Environmental Stance

At PyTea, we are committed to promoting sustainability and minimizing our environmental impact. One of the ways we achieve this is by prioritizing the use of eco-friendly and recyclable materials for our bubble tea cups. By opting for these materials, we reduce the amount of waste that ends up in landfills and contribute to a more circular economy.

Additionally, we have taken innovation a step further by incorporating insects into our special pearls. This unique approach not only adds an extra protein boost to our beverages but also indirectly helps decrease carbon emissions and water consumption. By embracing alternative protein sources, we reduce the environmental footprint associated with traditional animal agriculture. We believe that our small changes can make a big difference, and are proud to offer environmentally conscious options that contribute to a greener and more sustainable future.
